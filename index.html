About Joaquin

Joaquin is an intelligent assistant designed to help you create and organize calendar events effortlessly. By leveraging advanced AI technologies, Joaquin understands your schedule and manages your events so that you never miss an important appointment or meeting.

Simply tell Joaquin what you need, and it will handle everything from event scheduling to sending reminders. Say goodbye to manual calendar management and let Joaquin streamline your daily routine.
